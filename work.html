01 AI · RAG

RAG model for data analysis

A retrieval-augmented generation system that lets analysts ask natural-language questions against a body of documents and structured data, with answers grounded in the actual source material.

What it does

Indexes documents and datasets into a vector store, retrieves relevant chunks for any given question, and feeds them to an LLM to produce a grounded, citable answer.

Why it matters

Removes the hunt-and-peck stage of analysis. Stakeholders get answers from the data without needing to write queries or wait for a report.

  • Python
  • RAG
  • LLMs
  • Vector DB
  • Embeddings
